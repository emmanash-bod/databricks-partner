## Strategic Context

The deck "Why Great Partners Still Don't Get Referred" identifies four failure modes of boutique Databricks partners:

1. **Invisible to Databricks AEs** — no regular meetings, no visibility
2. **Generic Positioning** — undifferentiated, sounds like every other partner
3. **No Internal Engine** — no dedicated practice lead, no specific decks, nobody owns growth
4. **No Co-operative Sales Motion** — don't act as an extension of the Databricks sales team

This portal is designed to **solve all four problems** by giving Databricks reps a single destination that makes Blue Orange impossible to ignore, easy to position, and frictionless to sell alongside.

## Design & Branding Guidelines

### Visual Identity
- **Design approach:** A blend of Blue Orange Digital brand identity and the Databricks design language — similar to how Databricks styles their own assessment/partner-facing sites. The result should feel native to the Databricks ecosystem while clearly being Blue Orange's portal.
- **Primary palette:** Blue Orange brand colors (deep navy, vibrant orange) harmonized with Databricks' color system (Databricks red #FF3621 used as co-branded accent)
- **Typography:** Clean, modern sans-serif consistent with both brands
- **Photography style:** Use placeholder images throughout — real photos to be added later. Placeholders should use elegant geometric patterns, gradient backgrounds, or branded color blocks that look intentional, not broken.
- **Layout:** Clean, generous whitespace, card-based content sections
- **Mobile responsive:** AEs will access this on phones during customer meetings

### Design Principles
- **Speed over polish:** AEs need to find things in 10 seconds, not admire the design
- **Action-oriented:** Every section ends with a clear CTA
- **Scannable:** Heavy use of cards, icons, and bold headlines — AEs won't read paragraphs
- **Credibility signals throughout:** Logos, metrics, certifications in every view
- **Placeholder-ready:** All image and asset slots should have clean, branded placeholders that can be swapped out later without redesigning the layout
